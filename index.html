A About myself

Freelancing has always been a way to express myself outside of my normal job where I’m able to experiment with areas of design I don’t normally work. These projects range from creating collateral for the UN, designing apps and logo design. My traveling explorations and working as a freelancer have molded me into an independent self-starter, although I take pleasure in opportunities to work in group environments. As a result, I find collaborative work integral for success because of the diversity in backgrounds and perspective, I but realize self-direction and hard work are equally as important.
